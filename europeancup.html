The European cup is the pinnacle of European soccer. The best clubs in
Europe compete every year to see who is the best team in Europe.
Liverpool have been the most successful English club in this competition.
They have won this trophy 5 times in their history. They have also finished
runners up twice. Anfield on a European game night is a very special place indeed.
