My favourite player of all time is Kenny Dalglish. There have been many great players
to have put on the Liverpool jersey, but he is my favourite. Not only did he play for
Liverpool, but he also became a succesful manager of the club after the resignation of
Joe Fagan.
As a player Kenny won many trophies, including 6 league championships and 3 European cups
When he signed for Liverpool he cost a british transfer record fee, which will tell you how
good a player he was. He became the clubs player manager in 1985, and had alot of success in this
role also. After a very dark period in the clubs, and footballs history, Kenny Dalglish left his
role as club manager and took some time away from the game.
