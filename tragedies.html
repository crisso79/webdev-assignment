There has been 2 major tragedies in the history of Liverpool football club, that directly affected the club


Heysel
Heysel happened in the 1985 European cup final, when Liverpool were playing Juventus.
Liverpool fans crossed into the Juventus end of the stadium. The extra weight resulted in a wall collapsing and killing
39 fans. All Jventus fans. This led to a ban for English clubs from playing in European competitions

Hillsborough
Hillsborough was one of the saddest days in football history. But it was the saddest day in Liverpools history
96 fans died that day in an incient that could and should have benn prevented. Stadium safety and policing were at fault for
this tragedy. Families of the victims had to fight for almost 30 years to get justice for their loved ones
