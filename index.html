Liverpool football club was founded in 1892. The club was origianlly part of Everton FC,
but after a disagreement between management, a new club was formed, Liverpool F.C,
by John Houlding. The team originally wore a blue and white jersey, similar to the current
Everton F.C jersey.
This jersey was worn until 1894, when the team adopted the city's colour, red
Tom Watson was the first manager to have some success with the club. He won 3 league
titles and reacehd an FA cup final in his tenure. This happened before World War 1 started
After World War 1, the club won 2 more league championships before the 2nd world war.
When the war ended, they won one more league title and then entered a period of decline,
ending in relegation to the 2nd division in 1954
